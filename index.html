About us

At Chums we specialize in both delicious warm cheese -including Mont D’Or when it is in season from September to March - as well as fondue and baked Camembert.

Bring your friends, family or your loved ones, and we’ll make sure you have a taste experience out of the ordinary. You’ll find our winebars in Copenhagen near Nørreport st. and in Helsingør by Gammel Torv.
