Wine tasting

Location: Nørre Farimagsgade 82, 1364 København


Time and date: 28th of december 2025 at 5 pm.


Price: 350 kr. per person


Description:

Join us for a guided wine tasting at our Chums winebar located in the heart of Copenhagen. We will be tasting a curated selection of international wines paired with delicious cheese and charcuterie boards. Together, we dive deeper into the stories and origins of each bottle, and the different flavors that make them unique. This is a group activity, with a maximum of 10 participants. You'll be tasting a broad selection of 5 different wines, including red, white, natural and sweet varieties.
